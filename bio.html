Paul Casso

Paul Casso is a nom de plume, as one might guess. He is not a relative of a certain "Pi Casso", if one's imagination had begun to stray.

The artist has had some formal artistic training however, he is largely self-trained. He is mostly influenced by real world experiences, theatre, music, a lifetime of observations and disparate, exotic travels.

Paul Casso would like to share some of his more recent works with the rest of the world to bring joy, enlightenment, and possibly inspiration to others to take up a brush, pencil, charcoal, pen or just a marker and express themselves.
